How it works

Connected in under a minute.

Install on your computer

Download the macOS or Windows app above and drag it to your Applications folder. The app runs quietly in your menu bar / system tray.

Get the mobile app

Install TouchifyMouse on your phone from Google Play. Make sure your phone and computer are on the same Wi-Fi network.

Scan the QR code

Open the desktop app — a QR code appears. Tap "Scan QR" on the mobile app and point it at the screen. Pairing is instant.

Use your phone

Move the cursor with one finger, scroll with two, tap to click. Switch tabs at the bottom for keyboard, media, or audio modes.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does it cost anything?

The core app is free. Pro features (gyro mouse, custom workflows) are an optional one-time in-app purchase.

Does my data leave my network?

No. The phone and the computer talk directly over your local Wi-Fi. Trackpad input, keystrokes, and audio never touch our servers — we don't have any.

Why does the desktop app need Accessibility permission on macOS?

macOS requires Accessibility permission for any app that moves the cursor, types keystrokes, or sends media keys on your behalf. We only use it for that.

Phone-as-mic / remote speaker doesn't produce audio. Why?

Audio routing on macOS needs a free virtual audio driver called BlackHole. On Windows it needs VB-CABLE. The desktop app prompts you to install whichever is missing.

Can I use it over the internet, not just Wi-Fi?

Not currently. Both devices must be on the same local network. Internet relay is something we're considering, but it would mean adding a server and we like the local-only design for now.

Will it drain my phone battery?

Active use is similar to watching a video — the screen is the biggest draw. The app keeps the screen awake while in trackpad mode so you can keep using it without it locking.
